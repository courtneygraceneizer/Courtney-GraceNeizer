Hello! I’m Courtney-Grace, a fourth-year undergraduate student at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte with a concentration in bioinformatics.

I am passionate about empowering others through technology and education. I run The STEMinist Blog, a platform for young girls and women in STEM, promoting cross-cultural exchanges and empowering young girls in STEM.

I currently work in the Luo Lab (Bioinformatics) under Dr. Elaine Luo, in collaboration with Dr. Wenyu Gao (Statistics) to apply machine learning and statistical methods to microbial data analysis.
